Our Identity

The Student Experience

St Theresa Medical Training College provides a nurturing environment where faith meets science. As a constituent college of St Theresa Mission Hospital – Kiirua, our students are at the heart of a vibrant clinical ecosystem.

We focus on the holistic development of our learners, ensuring they graduate not only with technical skills but with the ethical foundation required to serve in the modern medical landscape.

Mission, Vision & Values

Our identity is guided by faith-based formation and service.

Mission

To train for service

To provide quality medical training rooted in faith, excellence, and compassionate care, preparing graduates to meet community health needs with professionalism and integrity.

Vision

Excellence in education

To be a leading faith-based medical training college recognised for strong academics, practical competence, and ethical formation.

Values

Our Pillars

  • ✅ Respect for life & dignity
  • ✅ Professional excellence
  • ✅ Service to community
  • ✅ Integrity & accountability

Clinical Training & Hospital Integration

Students gain supervised exposure to real clinical environments at St Theresa Mission Hospital, supporting the development of safe practice and professional conduct.

  • Structured clinical attachment and mentorship
  • Skills development through hands-on practice
  • Community outreach and service learning
