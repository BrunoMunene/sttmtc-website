Quality Milestone

STMH-K Laboratory Achieves International ISO 15189 Accreditation

📅 February 2026

We are proud to announce that the St Theresa Mission Hospital Laboratory has been officially accredited by KENAS (Kenya Accreditation Service). This prestigious ISO 15189:2012 certification recognizes our laboratory's commitment to international standards of quality and technical competence.

For our students at St Theresa MTC, this means training in a facility that guarantees efficiency, patient safety, and globally recognized service delivery. Special thanks to our dedicated Lab team and partners at NPHL, Global Fund, and AMREF for supporting this journey toward clinical excellence.
