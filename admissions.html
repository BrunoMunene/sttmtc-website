Admissions

Application guidance, entry requirements, and how to submit your documents.

Minimum Academic Requirements (KRCHN)

  • At least KCSE aggregate C (Plain)
  • Mandatory subjects: C in Biology; C in English or Kiswahili
  • Additional subjects: C- (Minus) in Physics, Chemistry, or Mathematics

How to Apply

  1. Choose a programme from the Courses page.
  2. Prepare required documents (ID, KCSE certificate/results slip, etc.).
  3. Submit via email or contact the admissions office.
  4. Await confirmation and reporting instructions.
